Our project

Haptic Feedback

Our goal is to create a way to feel more of the environment through the means of additional lightweight equipment to provide haptic feedback

Modular System

The end goal for our Haptic System is a fully modular and expandable system to allow users to customise their levels of feedback to their liking.

Virtual Reality

We aim to introduce our system as an addition to Virtual Reality technology to provide more immersive experiences for all users, allowing you to truly get lost in the virtual environments you discover.
